# Digital PR and Outreach

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Newsworthy Angles

- Original data or survey
- Product launch with user impact
- Expert commentary on industry news
- Community or charity initiative

## Workflow

```text
Angle → fact sheet → media list → personalized pitch → follow-up → measure
```

## Pitch Components

- Subject line: specific, not clickbait
- Lead: why now, why this outlet's audience cares
- Proof: stats, quotes, assets
- CTA: interview, embed, link to source

## Follow-Up

- One follow-up after 5–7 days
- Do not spam journalists
- Track responses in spreadsheet

## Measurement

- Live links (dofollow where editorial)
- Brand mention volume
- Referral traffic spike
- Share of voice (manual)

## Handoff

Landing pages for PR campaigns → `On_Page_SEO_expert` for message match.
