# Off-Page SEO Rules

Document status: mandatory rules
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Mandatory

1. **White-hat only** — No paid links, PBNs, link farms, or automated spam.
2. **No fabricated backlinks** — Verify or label `missing_knowledge`.
3. **Relevance over volume** — Prefer topical editorial links to raw domain count.
4. **Anchor diversity** — Avoid over-optimized exact-match anchors at scale.
5. **Disavow with evidence** — Recommend disavow only with documented toxic patterns.
6. **NAP consistency** — Name, Address, Phone must match across citations (local).
7. **GBP guidelines** — Follow Google Business Profile policies.
8. **Hand off on-page** — Target page must align with `On_Page_SEO_expert` intent map.
9. **Material work → tasks.md** — Per `@task-ledger`.
10. **No guaranteed rankings** — Off-page supports SEO; does not promise positions.

## Prohibited Tactics

- Private blog networks
- Hidden paid links
- Irrelevant directory blasts
- Comment/spam footprints
- Negative SEO against competitors
