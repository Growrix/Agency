# Social and Community SEO

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Role of Social

Social signals are **not** direct ranking factors like links—but they amplify content, drive discovery, and earn indirect links.

## Best Practices

- Complete profiles with consistent NAP and website URL
- Share linkable assets (research, tools, guides)
- Participate in relevant communities (Reddit, forums, Slack) with value-first approach
- Avoid spammy self-promotion

## Community SEO

- Answer questions with genuine expertise
- Link to canonical on-site resource when helpful
- Build reputation over time (E-E-A-T support)

## Measurement

- Referral traffic from social
- Assisted conversions (analytics)
- Mentions and links earned from community participation

## Boundaries

Paid social ads → out of scope. Organic community strategy only.
