# Influencer and Guest Posting

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Guest Posting Policy

Guest posts are acceptable when:

- Publisher is real, topical, and editorially controlled
- Content is original and valuable to their audience
- Links are contextual and not excessive
- No payment for links (paid sponsorship must be disclosed and often nofollow)

## Vetting Checklist

- [ ] Site has real audience (traffic proxy from user tools)
- [ ] Not a "write for us" link farm
- [ ] Editorial standards visible
- [ ] No obvious PBN patterns

## Influencer Collaboration

- Co-created content, webinars, podcasts
- Request show notes link to relevant resource
- UTM for tracking; link equity secondary to brand reach

## Deliverables

- Target publisher list criteria
- Content angle per publisher
- Author bio link policy
