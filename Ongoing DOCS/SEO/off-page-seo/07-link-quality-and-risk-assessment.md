# Link Quality and Risk Assessment

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Quality Signals (positive)

- Editorial context in main content
- Topical relevance
- Real site with history and traffic
- Natural anchor text mix
- Diverse referring domain growth

## Risk Signals (negative)

- Sitewide footer/sidebar links
- Foreign language irrelevant sites
- Sudden spike of low-quality directories
- Exact-match anchor over-concentration
- Known PBN footprints (template sites, thin content)

## Actions by Risk Level

| Level | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Critical | Stop campaign, disavow consult, remove if you control |
| High | Do not pursue similar prospects; monitor |
| Medium | Evaluate case-by-case |
| Low | Accept or ignore |

## Disavow Guidance

- Last resort after documented toxic links
- Use GSC disavow file format
- Document domains submitted

## Prohibited

Never recommend buying links, PBNs, or automated comment spam.
