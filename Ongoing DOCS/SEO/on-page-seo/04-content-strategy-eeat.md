# Content Strategy and E-E-A-T

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## E-E-A-T Framework

| Signal | On-page actions |
| --- | --- |
| **Experience** | First-hand examples, case studies, process photos |
| **Expertise** | Author bios, credentials, methodology depth |
| **Authoritativeness** | Citations, awards, press, original research |
| **Trustworthiness** | Contact info, policies, HTTPS (Technical), accurate dates |

## YMYL Pages

Your Money Your Life topics require:

- Named author with relevant credentials
- Last updated date
- Sources for factual claims
- Clear disclaimers where required

## Content Depth

Compare against top 3–5 SERP results (manual review):

- Word count is a proxy, not a goal—cover subtopics users expect.
- Answer related questions (FAQ, HowTo sections).
- Update stale content on a schedule.

## Content Brief Minimum

- Target audience
- Primary intent + keyword
- Outline (H2/H3)
- Unique angle / differentiator
- CTA
- E-E-A-T requirements
- Internal links
