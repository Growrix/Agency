# On-Page SEO Quality Gates

Document status: active
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Blocking (escalate before launch)

- Money pages with no clear primary keyword/intent
- Duplicate or missing H1 across indexable templates
- Title tags duplicated at scale on indexable routes
- Thin doorway pages targeting multiple unrelated intents
- Missing E-E-A-T on YMYL pages (health, finance, legal advice)
- No internal links to key conversion pages from main nav or hubs

## Non-Blocking (report, prioritize)

- Meta description length suboptimal
- Secondary keyword opportunities
- Heading hierarchy cosmetic issues
- Additional internal link suggestions
- Optional FAQ/content expansion

## Audit Severity Scale

| Level | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Critical | Indexable page likely to fail intent or quality expectations at scale |
| High | Material CTR or ranking risk on revenue pages |
| Medium | Optimization opportunity with moderate impact |
| Low | Minor polish |
| Informational | Best practice note, no action required |
