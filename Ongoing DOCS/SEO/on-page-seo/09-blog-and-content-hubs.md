# Blog and Content Hubs

Document status: domain guide
Last updated: 2026-07-14

## Hub Architecture

```text
Pillar page (broad topic, commercial tie-in)
  ├── Cluster article A (subtopic)
  ├── Cluster article B
  └── Cluster article C
```

## Pillar Page Requirements

- Comprehensive coverage of topic
- Links to all cluster posts
- Clear CTA to product/service
- Unique title/H1 targeting head term or topic

## Cluster Articles

- Narrower intent, long-tail keywords
- Link up to pillar and sideways to related clusters
- Avoid duplicating pillar content—add unique angle
