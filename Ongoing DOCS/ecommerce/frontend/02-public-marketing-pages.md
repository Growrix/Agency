# Public Marketing Pages

Document status: frontend source
Owner: Marketing and frontend

## Purpose

Define public pages that build trust, capture demand, and support SEO.

## Required Pages

- Home.
- Landing pages.
- About.
- Contact.
- FAQ.
- Privacy policy.
- Terms.
- Refund policy.
- Shipping policy.
- Blog listing and post detail.
- Careers.
- Affiliate.
- Store locator when physical locations exist.

## Page Rules

- Each public page must have SEO title, description, canonical URL, Open Graph, and structured data where relevant.
- Trust and policy pages should be linkable from product, cart, and checkout surfaces.
- Contact paths should route to support, sales, WhatsApp, email, or ticketing depending on intent.
- Landing pages should connect campaign source, offer, product/category, and checkout path.

## Content Blocks

- Value proposition.
- Featured categories/products.
- Social proof and reviews.
- Shipping, return, payment, and guarantee trust signals.
- FAQ and support routes.
- Campaign-specific CTA blocks.

## Analytics Events

- `marketing.page_viewed`
- `marketing.cta_clicked`
- `support.contact_started`
- `newsletter.signup_submitted`
- `affiliate.intent_started`

## Acceptance Criteria

- Public pages support SEO, trust, and conversion goals.
- Policy content is visible before and during checkout.
- Contact and support routes are clear for pre-purchase and post-purchase users.
