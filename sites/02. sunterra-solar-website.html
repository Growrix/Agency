FAQ

Common questions

How much do solar panels cost in Australia in 2026?

A quality 6.6 kW residential system typically ranges from $5,500–$9,500 AUD after STC rebates, depending on panel tier, inverter brand, roof complexity and state.

What are STCs and how do they reduce my price?

Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) are created when eligible solar is installed. Your retailer claims them upfront as a point-of-sale discount based on system size, postcode zone factor and deeming period under the RET.

Do I need CEC accreditation?

Yes — only Clean Energy Council (CEC) accredited designers and installers can claim STCs and connect systems under Australian standards. Sunterra is fully CEC accredited.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installs complete in 1–2 days after DNSP approval. From contract to switch-on, allow 4–8 weeks for approvals and inspection.

Will solar work during a blackout?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down for safety. With a battery and backup gateway, essential or whole-home circuits can stay powered.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about solar, STCs, CEC accreditation and the NEM.

How much do solar panels cost in Australia in 2026?

A quality 6.6 kW residential system typically ranges from $5,500–$9,500 AUD after STC rebates, depending on panel tier, inverter brand, roof complexity and state.

What are STCs and how do they reduce my price?

Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) are created when eligible solar is installed. Your retailer claims them upfront as a point-of-sale discount based on system size, postcode zone factor and deeming period under the RET.

Do I need CEC accreditation?

Yes — only Clean Energy Council (CEC) accredited designers and installers can claim STCs and connect systems under Australian standards. Sunterra is fully CEC accredited.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installs complete in 1–2 days after DNSP approval. From contract to switch-on, allow 4–8 weeks for approvals and inspection.

Will solar work during a blackout?

Standard grid-tied systems shut down for safety. With a battery and backup gateway, essential or whole-home circuits can stay powered.

What feed-in tariff will I get?

FiTs vary by retailer and state across the NEM. We model export using current market rates and recommend retailers aligned with your usage.

Is finance available?

Yes — Sunterra partners with Australian green loan providers offering $0 upfront options subject to approval.

What warranty do you offer?

25-year panel performance, 10–15 year inverter warranties, plus Sunterra's 10-year workmanship guarantee.

Can I add a battery later?

Most systems can be retrofitted. We design with future AC coupling in mind.

Do you service commercial sites?

Yes — from 10 kW cafés to 500 kW warehouses with NEM tariff analysis and three-phase connections.

Which states do you install in?

We install across NSW, VIC, QLD, SA, WA, TAS, NT and ACT with local CEC-accredited crews.

How do I claim state battery rebates?

Eligibility varies — Solar Victoria, NSW battery incentive and other programs have specific requirements we verify during your quote.
