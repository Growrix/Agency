# AI-BOS Overview

Human-facing overview of the AI Business Operating System.

AI-BOS is a tool-independent business operating system. It separates Knowledge, Agents, MCP, Projects, and Runtime so the business foundation survives vendor and tool changes.

- AR-AI-BOS-001 — Overall Vision
- AR-AI-BOS-012 — Standards, Templates, Evolution Strategy, and Repository Structure
