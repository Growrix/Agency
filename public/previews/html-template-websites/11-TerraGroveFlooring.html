How it works

From first look to final walkthrough.

A calm, five-step path. You'll always know what happens next, who's coming, and what it costs — before any work begins.

Start with a free visit
Step 01

Room assessment

We look at light, subfloor, traffic and moisture to recommend materials that will actually thrive in your space — not just look good in a showroom.

Step 02

Material selection

Take home samples, preview finishes in the visualizer, and compare warmth, durability and budget side by side until one feels unmistakably right.

Step 03

Measurement & planning

Laser-measured to the inch, with layout, transitions and waste allowance mapped out. You approve a fixed quote and an installation date — no surprises.

Step 04

Professional installation

Certified craftsmen prep the subfloor, protect your home, and lay every plank and tile with the patience it deserves. Clean site, every day.

Step 05

Final walkthrough

We inspect together, hand over care guidance and warranty paperwork, and don't call it done until you'd happily show the room to anyone.

Built to outlast trends

The difference is under the surface.

Beautiful floors fail when the prep is rushed. Our four-stage standard is why ours don't.

01

Precision preparation

Every job starts below the surface. We test moisture, level the subfloor and correct flaws that would otherwise telegraph through your new floor.

  • Moisture & humidity testing
  • Subfloor leveling to ±2mm
02

Expert installation

Certified craftsmen who lay floors for a living — not a side job. Tight seams, balanced layouts and clean transitions, every plank and tile.

  • NWFA & CTEF certified installers
  • Dust-controlled, protected worksite
03

Material protection

Premium materials deserve premium handling. We acclimate, store and seal everything correctly so the floor you chose is the floor you keep.

  • 48–72hr on-site acclimation
  • Manufacturer-spec sealing
04

Warranty coverage

We stand behind the work in writing. Installation is warranted for 25 years, with material warranties from the makers we partner with.

  • 25-year installation warranty
  • Lifetime repair support
