FAQ

Common questions.

Can't find your answer? Call us — we pick up.

(512) 555-0182
How long does flooring installation take?
Most residential projects finish in 2–5 days depending on square footage, material and subfloor prep required. We give you a precise timeline in writing before starting.
Which flooring is best for kitchens?
Porcelain tile and luxury vinyl are our top recommendations — fully waterproof, easy to clean and rated for heavy traffic. We'll bring samples to show you in your own kitchen light.
Do you remove existing flooring?
Yes. Removal and disposal is an add-on service priced per square foot. We'll assess your existing floor and include it in your written quote.
Are materials included in the price?
Our quotes include everything — material, underlayment, adhesive, trims and labor. No line items appear on the day of installation that weren't in your written estimate.
Is financing available?
Yes — we work with several financing partners offering 0% promotional periods. Ask us at your consultation and we'll walk you through the options.
What warranties do you offer?
We offer a 10-year installation warranty covering workmanship, plus manufacturer material warranties registered in your name at project completion. Lifetime repair support is included.
