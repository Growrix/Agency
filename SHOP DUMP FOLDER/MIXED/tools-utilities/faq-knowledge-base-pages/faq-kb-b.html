📦 Orders & Tracking

How do I track my order?+
You'll receive an SMS and email with a tracking link once your order is dispatched. You can also track it in the ShopMart app under Orders → Track. Courier tracking updates every 2–3 hours.
Can I cancel my order after placing it?+
Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement if they haven't been dispatched. Go to My Orders → Cancel. After dispatch, you'll need to initiate a return upon delivery. We cannot cancel orders already out for delivery.
What are the delivery times?+
Dhaka & major cities: 1–2 business days. Other areas: 2–4 business days. Same-day delivery is available for select Dhaka areas on orders placed before 12PM. Express delivery is ৳80 extra and available 9AM–9PM daily.
My order is showing 'delivered' but I haven't received it.+
Please check with family members or building security first. If still not found, contact us within 24 hours of the delivery timestamp — we'll investigate with the courier and arrange a replacement or refund if delivery is confirmed as failed.

🔄 Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?+
Items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached. Fashion items are eligible for 14 days. We cover return courier costs for defective or wrong items. For change-of-mind returns, ৳60 courier deducted from refund.
How long does a refund take?+
Once we receive the returned item: bKash/Nagad refunds process in 1–2 hours. Card refunds take 5–7 business days depending on your bank. Wallet credit (ShopMart balance) is instant and you get an extra 5% as a goodwill credit.
