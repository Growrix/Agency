Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your appointment — booking, pricing, cancellations, and aftercare.

📅Booking & Appointments
How do I book an appointment?+
You can book through our website using the Book Appointment button, via our Instagram (@theblossomstudio), or by calling us on 01700-XXXXXX. We recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance, especially for weekends. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.
Do you accept walk-in clients?+
Yes — walk-ins are welcome during opening hours, but appointments are always prioritised. On Fridays and Saturdays, walk-in waits can be 30–90 minutes. We recommend calling ahead on 01700-XXXXXX to check same-day availability before coming in.
What is your cancellation policy?+
We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice incur a ৳300 fee. Same-day no-shows are charged ৳500. We understand emergencies happen — just let us know as soon as you can and we'll do our best to work with you.
How long should I allow for my appointment?+
Blow-out: 45–60 min. Haircut: 30–45 min. Full colour with cut: 2.5–3.5 hours. Balayage or highlights: 3–4 hours. Keratin treatment: 3 hours. We always build in a little extra time so we're never rushing — please don't book other plans immediately after a colour appointment.
💳Pricing & Payments
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept cash, bKash, Nagad, and all major debit/credit cards. We do not currently accept bank transfers for individual services. A 10% service charge applies to all bookings — this is shared among our team and is not optional.
Why are prices not listed on your website?+
Hair services vary significantly based on length, thickness, and the specific technique involved. A haircut for short hair and for thick, long hair require very different time and product. We provide a quote during your consultation so you know the exact price before any service begins — no surprises.
Do you offer student or loyalty discounts?+
Yes — students receive 15% off all services on production of a valid student ID (Tuesday–Thursday only, excludes weekends). Our Blossom Loyalty Card gives you a free blow-out after 8 services. Cards are issued at reception — ask your stylist to stamp it after each visit.
✂️Services & Aftercare
Do you offer a guarantee on your services?+
Absolutely. If you're unhappy with your cut or colour, contact us within 7 days and we'll invite you back for a complimentary adjustment. This applies to any concerns about the technique — not fading caused by home washing routines. We want every client to leave loving their hair.
How should I care for my colour at home?+
Use sulphate-free, colour-safe shampoo — we sell our preferred brands in-studio. Wash with lukewarm water (hot water fades colour faster). For the first 48 hours after colouring: avoid washing, heavy sweating, and swimming. Apply a weekly deep conditioning mask. We recommend products at your appointment based on your specific colour treatment.
