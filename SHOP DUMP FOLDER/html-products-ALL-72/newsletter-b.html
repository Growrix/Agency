📚 Currently Reading

The Book That Slowed Me Down

"How to Do Nothing" — Jenny Odell

A manifesto against the attention economy. Odell argues that resistance doesn't look like protest — it looks like paying attention to where you already are. This book made me put my phone down for an entire Saturday.

I've been sitting with one quote all week: "What I'm proposing is not a retreat into total silence but rather a strategic slowing down — the ability to navigate multiple temporalities and know what kind of time you are in."

🍜 In the Kitchen

Miso Butter Noodles — my new Wednesday ritual

You'll need

· Noodles of any kind
· 2 tbsp white miso
· 1 tbsp butter
· Soy sauce + sesame oil
· Chilli flakes + a soft egg
· Whatever herbs you have

The method

Cook noodles, save a little water. Mix miso + butter + soy in the pot with the noodle water. Toss noodles through. Top with everything. 15 minutes, total comfort.
