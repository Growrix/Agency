FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — Ayan Architects has been named IEB Architectural Project of the Year 2025 for the Sylhet Community Library. Ceremony: 14 June.‌ 

Press Release

Institute of Engineers, Bangladesh

22 May 2025

For Immediate Release

Award Announcement

Ayan Architects Named
Project of the Year
2025.

     

The Institute of Engineers, Bangladesh is honoured to announce that Ayan Architects has been awarded the IEB Architectural Project of the Year 2025 for the design and execution of the Sylhet Community Library — a landmark in civic architecture that has been described by the judging panel as "a building that belongs to its city in a way few public structures do."

The project was selected from 84 nominations across eight categories. The jury cited Ayan Architects' use of locally sourced stone, passive cooling systems inspired by traditional Bengali architecture, and the community consultation process that informed every major design decision.

"In a decade of entries, this is the first building we awarded unanimously. It is both humble and extraordinary."

Ar. Dilruba Sultana, Chair · IEB Award Jury 2025
