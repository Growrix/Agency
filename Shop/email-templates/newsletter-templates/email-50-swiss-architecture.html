01 · Residential

Baridhara Residence

A 4,200 sqft family home built around a central courtyard. The programme inverts expectation: living rooms below, private retreats above, with a rooftop terrace bridging both.

02 · Cultural

Mirpur Cultural Centre

Public space conceived as a threshold. The building sits at the edge of a park and a market — porous to both. Exposed brick, steel, and bamboo form a material vocabulary of its neighbourhood.

03 · Retreat

Cox's Bazar Coastal Retreat

Six cabins arranged along a coastal ridge, each oriented toward the sea. The structure is deliberately minimal: timber, concrete, and glass that refuses to compete with the landscape it inhabits.
