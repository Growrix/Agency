            The super shop that sweats the details

            Primora started with a simple frustration: why does "convenient" always mean "compromised"?

            In 2017, three friends — a grocer's daughter, a logistics engineer and a pastry chef — opened a 40-square-metre shop with one rule: never stock anything we wouldn't take home ourselves. That rule never changed. The shop just got bigger.

            Today Primora serves over 120,000 households across 42 delivery zones, with 18,500 products spanning fresh produce, pantry staples, electronics, beauty, baby care and everything between. We source produce within 24 hours of harvest, run our own cold-chain fleet, and taste-test every private-label product before it earns shelf space.

            We're stubborn about three things: freshness you can taste, prices that are honest every day (not just on sale days), and service that treats your order like our own family's shop.

            What we stand for

            Our promises, in writing

            • Freshness, audited daily

              Our quality team rejects roughly 4% of inbound produce every morning. You only ever see the rest.

            • Honest everyday pricing

              No inflated "was" prices. Every discount is a genuine markdown from the everyday shelf price.

            • People first, always

              Every Primora picker, driver and support agent is employed directly — trained, insured and paid fairly.

            Frequently asked questions

            Everything shoppers ask us most — delivery, returns, payments and rewards.

            How fast is Primora delivery?

            Orders placed before 2 PM qualify for same-day delivery in metro zones. Standard delivery arrives within 1–2 business days, and Primora Plus members get free priority delivery on every order.

            What is the return policy?

            Non-perishable items can be returned within 30 days in original condition for a full refund. Fresh and frozen items are covered by our Freshness Promise — report an issue within 24 hours for an instant replacement or refund. Full details on the return policy page.

            Is there a minimum order for free shipping?

            Shipping is free on all orders over $75. Primora Plus and Elite members enjoy free delivery with no minimum. Below the threshold, delivery starts at $4.90 in metro zones.

            How does the loyalty program work?

            Every dollar spent earns 2 Primora Points. Points convert to store credit automatically at 500-point milestones, and members unlock early access to flash sales and seasonal events.

            Which payment methods do you accept?

            Visa, Mastercard and American Express (with 3-D Secure), digital wallet balance, and cash on delivery in all zones. Card details are tokenised — we never store raw card numbers.

            How is cold-chain freshness protected in transit?

            Chilled and frozen items travel in insulated totes with temperature loggers. If a logger records a breach, we proactively replace the item — usually before you even notice.

            Can I change or cancel an order after placing it?

            Yes — orders can be edited or cancelled free of charge until they enter picking, typically within 30 minutes of placing. After that, our support team can still intercept most orders before dispatch.

            Do you offer business or bulk accounts?

            We do. Primora for Business offers wholesale pricing, consolidated invoicing and scheduled standing orders. Contact our team via the contact page and choose "Something else".

            Privacy policy

            Last updated: 1 June 2026

            Primora ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. It applies to primora.example.com, our mobile apps and our delivery services.

            What we collect

            • Account data — name, email, phone number and delivery addresses you provide.
            • Order data — items purchased, order value, delivery zone and fulfilment history.
            • Device data — browser type, approximate region and interaction events used to keep the service fast and secure.

            How we use it

            • To fulfil orders, process payments and deliver to the right door.
            • To personalise recommendations and remember your basket between visits.
            • To send service messages, and — only with consent — marketing offers you can opt out of with one click.
            • To detect fraud and keep the platform secure.

            What we never do

            We never sell your personal data. We never share your shopping history with advertisers in identifiable form. Card numbers are tokenised by our PCI-DSS-compliant payment processor and never touch our servers.

            Cookies and local storage

            We use strictly-necessary local storage to keep your cart, wishlist and theme preference on your own device. Analytics cookies are optional and disabled until you opt in.

            Your rights

            You can request a copy, correction or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@primora.example.com. We respond within 30 days and verify every request.

            Data retention

            Order records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance. Account data is deleted within 60 days of a verified deletion request, except where law requires otherwise.

            Contact

            Data Protection Officer, Primora, 128 Harvest Lane, Greenfield District — or privacy@primora.example.com.

            Shipping policy

            Last updated: 1 June 2026

            Zones and timing

            • Metro city — $4.90. Same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM; otherwise next morning.
            • Suburban — $6.90. Next-day delivery, 7 AM – 9 PM windows.
            • Nationwide — $9.90. 1–2 business days via our insulated courier network.

            Free shipping

            Orders over $75 ship free in every zone. Primora Plus and Elite members always ship free, with Elite unlocking 2-hour express windows in metro zones.

            Cold chain

            Chilled and frozen items are packed in insulated totes with ice packs and temperature loggers. Totes are collected on your next delivery — or leave them out and we'll grab them.

            Missed deliveries

            We follow your delivery notes first. If we can't deliver safely, we'll attempt redelivery once free of charge, then hold your order for pickup at the nearest hub for 48 hours (perishables excluded).

            Tracking

            Every order gets live GPS tracking from dispatch, plus SMS updates at picking, dispatch and arrival. Track anytime from your orders.

            2. Return policy

            Return policy

            Last updated: 1 June 2026

            Non-perishable items — 30 days

            Electronics, fashion, beauty, home and pantry goods can be returned within 30 days of delivery in original, resalable condition. Start a return from your orders — we'll send a courier to collect at no charge for items over $30.

            Fresh and frozen — the Freshness Promise

            If anything perishable arrives below our standard, report it within 24 hours with a photo. We issue an instant replacement or refund — no questions, no return shipping, keep or compost the item.

            Exclusions

            • Opened personal-care items (hygiene) unless faulty.
            • Gift cards and digital memberships.
            • Custom-cut deli, meat and fish prepared to order — covered by the Freshness Promise instead.

            Refund timing

            Approved refunds reach your original payment method within 3–5 business days. Wallet refunds are instant. Reward points used on a refunded order are re-credited automatically.

            Faulty items

            Statutory rights apply on top of this policy. Faulty electronics within warranty are repaired, replaced or refunded — your choice — including return shipping.
