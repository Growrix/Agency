The Aurelhaus Process

Seven steps, one extraordinary home

A clear, communicated path from first conversation to move-in day — with a dedicated project lead and fixed checkpoints at every stage.

01
Weeks 1–2

Discovery

We listen first. A complimentary consultation to understand your vision, lifestyle, must-haves, and the life you want your home to make possible.

02
Weeks 2–4

Land Evaluation

Own land or still searching? We assess site orientation, topography, views, and zoning — or help you find the parcel that fits the home you want.

03
Months 1–3

Design Development

Our architects translate your vision into floor plans, elevations, and 3D walkthroughs — refined collaboratively until it feels unmistakably yours.

04
Month 3

Budget Planning

A transparent, line-item budget with fixed allowances. No surprises, no hidden costs — every selection and its impact laid out in plain terms.

05
Months 4–14

Construction

Master craftsmen build to exacting standards with weekly updates, a live project portal, and your dedicated project lead on call throughout.

06
Final month

Final Walkthrough

A meticulous, room-by-room review against our quality checklist — every finish, fixture, and detail confirmed to your standard before handover.

07
Move-in day

Move-In Celebration

The keys are yours. We celebrate the moment, hand over your home-care plan and warranty, and begin a relationship that lasts well beyond the build.
