How long does window installation take?
Most residential window replacements complete in one day. Bay windows, bow windows and whole-home projects typically run two to three days. We provide your exact schedule in writing before starting.
Are your products energy efficient?
Yes — all our core range is ENERGY STAR® certified. We offer double and triple-pane glass options with low-E coatings and argon-gas fills that can cut heat transfer by up to 70% versus single-pane windows.
Do you offer financing?
We do. We work with GreenSky, Synchrony and Wells Fargo Home Projects to offer 12–60 month plans with 0% promotional periods for qualified applicants. Use our calculator above to estimate your monthly payment.
What warranties are included?
All installations carry our lifetime workmanship warranty. Product warranties are provided by the manufacturer — typically 20–30 years on vinyl and aluminium frames, and 10–20 years on glass units. Every warranty is registered in your name at project completion.
Can I customise colours and finishes?
Absolutely. We offer 30+ exterior and interior colour options, multiple hardware finishes and glass choices including obscured, tinted, laminated and decorative options. Our consultants bring full sample sets to your home.
Do I need permits for window replacement?
Like-for-like window replacements generally don't require permits in most jurisdictions. Structural changes, enlarging openings or adding new windows typically do. We handle all permit applications on your behalf and include the cost in your written quote.
