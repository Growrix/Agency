FAQ

Common questions answered

Everything you need to know about our clinic, appointments and services.

Contact us
How do I book an appointment?

Use our online booking system to choose your specialty, doctor, date and time. You can also call us on +1 (800) 555-0100 or visit our front desk. Same-day appointments are usually available.

Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance providers including BlueCross BlueShield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealth and Medicare. Please call us to verify your specific plan before your visit.

How does telemedicine work?

After booking a virtual appointment, you'll receive a secure link to join a video consultation with your doctor. You'll need a device with camera and microphone. Prescriptions and referrals can be issued digitally.

Can I access my medical records online?

Yes. All patients have access to our Patient Portal where you can view lab results, prescriptions, consultation notes and billing history — securely, from any device.

What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant previous medical records. New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration.

How long does it take to get lab results?

Most routine blood tests are available within 24 hours. Imaging results are typically ready in 1–2 business days. Critical results are communicated to you and your doctor immediately.
