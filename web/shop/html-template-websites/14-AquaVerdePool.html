FAQ

Common questions.

Ask us anything — we answer the phone.

(512) 555-0210
How often should my pool be cleaned?
Most residential pools in warm climates need weekly cleaning. Pools with heavy use, surrounded by trees or in hot humid areas may need bi-weekly service. We assess your specific pool and recommend the right schedule at your free inspection.
Do you provide the chemicals?
Standard and Premium plans include all chemicals — chlorine, pH adjusters, alkalinity and stabiliser. Basic plan customers purchase their own chemicals and we do the testing and dosing guidance. No hidden costs.
What's included in maintenance plans?
All plans include skim, brush and vacuum at every visit, water testing, a digital service report with photos, and basic equipment health monitoring. Standard and Premium add chemical balancing, filter cleaning, and priority response. Premium includes all chemicals and 24/7 emergency priority.
Can you fix pool equipment?
Yes — we repair and replace pumps, motors, filters, heaters, salt cells, automation systems and plumbing. We're factory-trained on Pentair, Hayward, Jandy and Zodiac equipment and carry common parts in our service vehicles.
Do you service saltwater pools?
Absolutely — saltwater pools are a speciality. We monitor salinity, cell output, pH and cyanuric acid at every visit and can clean, test or replace salt cells. We've been servicing saltwater systems since they became mainstream.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
Our emergency line is answered 24/7. For urgent issues like pump failures, severe leaks or complete green-water recovery, we aim to have a technician on site within 4–6 hours. Premium plan members receive priority same-day dispatch.
