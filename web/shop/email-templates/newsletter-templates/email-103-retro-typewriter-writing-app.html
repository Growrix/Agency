[NEW] FEATURE 01

Distraction-Free Mode

Full screen. No toolbar. No notifications. Just you and your words on a clean white page. Press F11 or ⌘+Shift+F to enter. Press anything else to stay in the writing.
 

[NEW] FEATURE 02

Typewriter Click Sounds

Six authentic typewriter sound packs — 1920s Oliver, 1950s Hermes, 1970s IBM. Each keystroke becomes satisfying. Works with headphones. Volume adjustable per character type.
 

[NEW] FEATURE 03

Daily Word Goal Tracker

Set a daily target. See a quiet progress bar grow as you write. Streak counter visible only when you've hit your goal. No guilt, just numbers.
