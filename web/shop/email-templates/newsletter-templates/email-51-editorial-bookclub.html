This Month's Selection

"The Buried Giant"

by Kazuo Ishiguro

"Can love survive when the memories that shaped it disappear?"

Set in post-Arthurian Britain, Ishiguro's novel follows an elderly couple on a journey through a land afflicted by collective amnesia. At once a quest narrative, a meditation on memory, and a quiet, devastating love story — this book earns every slow sentence.
