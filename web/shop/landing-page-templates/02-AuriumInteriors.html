Design Philosophy

Architecture is the
quiet art of orchestrating light,
material, and human ritual.

— 01
Function
Every choice begins with how a space will be lived in — daily rituals, light cycles, acoustic comfort.
— 02
Emotion
Rooms should make you feel something the moment you enter. Atmosphere is the architecture you cannot see.
— 03
Light
Both natural and artificial — choreographed across the day. Lighting is half of every space we design.
— 04
Material
Honest, refined, and tactile. We curate surfaces that reward touch and improve with time.
— 05
Human
Spaces shaped by the people who inhabit them. The brief drives form — never the other way around.

Common Questions

Considered answers

Our most asked questions on engagement, timeline, fees, and collaboration.

Ask a question
How do projects typically begin?

Every engagement begins with a discovery consultation — either at your space or our studio. We review your brief, lifestyle, and aspirations, then prepare a detailed scope and fee proposal within 7 working days.

What's the typical project timeline?

A full residential interior runs 6–18 months from brief to handover, depending on scale and structural work. Commercial briefs vary from 4–12 months. We provide a Gantt timeline with every proposal.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. Our studios are based in London and Los Angeles, but active projects span 14 countries. We travel monthly for site visits and partner with vetted local contractors in each market.

What's your fee structure?

We offer three engagement models: fixed-fee design (concept through specifications), full turnkey (design + procurement + project management), and hourly consultancy. All fees are agreed upfront — no commissions, no markup surprises.

Can you work with my architect or contractor?

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with the client's existing architects, contractors, and consultants. Clear communication protocols and shared documentation keep all parties aligned.

How involved am I during the process?

As involved as you wish. Most clients meet with us monthly for key milestone reviews; others prefer weekly updates. Our project portal gives you live access to plans, schedules, and budget at any moment.
