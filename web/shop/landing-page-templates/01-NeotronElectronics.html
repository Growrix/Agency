Frequently Asked

Got questions?

Quick answers on shipping, warranty, returns, compatibility and payment.

Contact Support
How long is shipping?

We ship globally to 120+ countries via DHL Express and FedEx Priority. Most orders arrive in 2–4 business days. Free shipping on all orders over $99.

What's covered by warranty?

Every Neotron device includes a 2-year international warranty covering manufacturing defects, battery health (>80% capacity), and software issues. Accidental damage is covered with Neotron Care+.

What's your return policy?

30-day no-questions-asked returns. Products must be in original condition. Free return shipping in the US, EU, UK and Australia. International returns are pre-paid via QR label.

Are products compatible with iOS and Android?

Yes. Our ecosystem works seamlessly with iOS 16+ and Android 11+. Pulse Pro runs Neotron OS (Android-based) with full Google services.

What payment options do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, Klarna (4 interest-free payments), and Affirm financing for orders over $500.

Can I trade in my old device?

Yes. We offer instant trade-in credit on every order. Get up to $850 toward a Pulse Pro or up to $200 toward audio products. Free shipping label included.
