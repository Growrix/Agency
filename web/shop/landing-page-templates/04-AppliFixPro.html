Symptom Guide

Common Appliance Problems

Recognize these symptoms? Our technicians resolve these issues every day.

Refrigerator Not Cooling

Could indicate a failed compressor, condenser fan motor, or thermostat. Food safety risk — needs urgent attention.

Urgent

Washer Not Draining

Often caused by a clogged pump filter, failed drain pump, or kinked hose. Can lead to mold and odors if left untreated.

Common Fix

Dryer Not Heating

Usually a thermal fuse, heating element, or igniter issue. Cycles run but clothes stay damp. Quick same-day repair.

Same-Day Fix

Dishwasher Leaking

Worn door gasket, cracked spray arm, or faulty pump seal. Water damage risk makes this a priority repair.

Priority

Oven Not Igniting

Faulty igniter, gas valve issue, or control board malfunction. Safety concern — discontinue use and call immediately.

Safety Issue

Ice Maker Failure

Water inlet valve, thermostat, or motor module issue. Often resolved with a single part replacement visit.
