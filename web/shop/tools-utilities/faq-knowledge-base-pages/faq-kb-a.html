Billing & Subscriptions

18 articles · Last updated May 2025
How do I upgrade from Free to a paid plan?+
Go to Settings → Billing and click Upgrade Plan. Choose Growth or Enterprise, enter your payment details, and your new features will be available immediately. If you upgrade mid-month, you'll only be charged a pro-rated amount for the remaining days. View our pricing page →
What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and local bank transfers for annual plans. For Bangladeshi customers, bKash and Nagad are available for monthly plans. Bank transfer instructions are emailed upon request — contact support@nucleus.io.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?+
Yes. Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan. Your account downgrades to Free at the end of your current billing period — you won't lose access mid-cycle. We don't offer refunds for partial months, but annual plan refunds are evaluated case by case.
How do I get an invoice for my subscription?+
All invoices are automatically emailed to your billing email after each charge. You can also download past invoices from Settings → Billing → Invoice History. If you need a company-specific invoice with custom billing details, use the Update Billing Info option before your next renewal.
What happens if my payment fails?+
We retry failed payments up to 3 times over 7 days. During this period, your account remains active. If payment still fails after 7 days, your account is downgraded to Free. You'll receive email notifications throughout — update your card in Settings → Billing → Payment Method.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or students?+
Yes. We offer a 50% discount for registered non-profits and a free Growth plan for students at accredited institutions. Email support@nucleus.io with proof of eligibility (charity registration number or student ID). Processing takes 1–2 business days.
