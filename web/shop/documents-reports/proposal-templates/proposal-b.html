01 · Understanding

Where Botanic is today.

Botanic Wellness is entering a competitive Dhaka wellness market with genuinely superior products — but the brand currently doesn't reflect that quality. The current visual identity feels generic, inconsistent across channels, and fails to communicate the premium, nature-rooted positioning you've articulated in our conversations.

The opportunity is significant. The wellness market in Bangladesh is growing at 24% YoY, and premium positioning is undercrowded. The brand you build now will define how you're perceived for the next decade.

The Challenge

Existing identity lacks consistency, premium feel, and clear visual differentiation from commodity wellness brands.

The Opportunity

Be the first Bangladeshi wellness brand that feels internationally refined while remaining culturally rooted.
