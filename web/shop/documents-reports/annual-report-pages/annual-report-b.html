A Letter from the Founder

A year we didn't expect, and wouldn't undo.

2024 was the year Bondhu Foundation grew faster than we planned — and slower than communities needed. We reached 4,840 families across Dhaka, Sylhet, and Cox's Bazar. We also ran short of funds twice, paused one programme, and had to make difficult choices about who gets help when the list is long and resources are finite.

This report tells both stories — the impact we're proud of, and the gaps that still exist. We owe that honesty to every donor who trusted us with their money, and every family who trusted us with their dignity.
