Common Questions

Are standing desks worth it?

If you sit 6+ hours a day, yes. Research consistently shows alternating between sitting and standing reduces back pain and fatigue. The key is alternating — not standing all day.

How long should I stand?

The rule of thumb: 20 minutes sitting, 8 minutes standing, 2 minutes moving. A good desk makes this effortless with memory presets.
