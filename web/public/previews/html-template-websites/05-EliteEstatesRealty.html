FAQ

Common questions

Answers to what our global clients ask us most often.

Speak with an agent
How do you access off-market listings?

Our global network of agents and developer relationships gives us access to properties that never reach public portals. We present off-market opportunities exclusively to qualified clients who register with us.

Do you assist international buyers?

Absolutely. We operate in 60+ countries and have multilingual agents, international legal partners, and currency specialists who guide overseas clients through every step of a cross-border purchase.

What investment returns can I expect?

Returns vary by asset class and location. Our managed investment properties have delivered 6–12% annual yields. We provide detailed ROI modelling before any acquisition recommendation.

How does the property search process work?

We begin with a confidential consultation to understand your requirements and budget. Our team then curates a shortlist, arranges private viewings, and manages all negotiations on your behalf.

Do you offer property management?

Yes. Our property management division handles tenant sourcing, vetting, rent collection, maintenance and compliance reporting — giving investors a completely hands-off experience.

What documentation is required to buy?

Requirements vary by country. Typically you'll need proof of identity, proof of funds, and a mortgage agreement if applicable. Our legal team coordinates all documentation and due diligence.
