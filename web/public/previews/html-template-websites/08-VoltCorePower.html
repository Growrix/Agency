FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers to what customers ask about our electrical services and pricing.

More questions
Do you offer 24/7 emergency electrical service?

Yes. Our emergency electricians are on-call 24/7, 365 days a year. Most emergency calls receive an on-site response within 60 minutes.

How much does electrical service cost?

All pricing is flat-rate and upfront. Common services start at $129 (diagnostic) — most repairs $250–$800. EV chargers from $1,200 fully installed. Free estimates on installations over $500.

Are your electricians licensed?

Yes. All VoltCore electricians are state-licensed Master or Journeyman level, fully insured, background-checked and continuously trained on the latest NEC code updates.

How long does an EV charger installation take?

A standard Level 2 home installation takes 3–5 hours. If a panel upgrade is required, allow 6–8 hours. We coordinate any permits and inspections.

Do you offer financing?

Yes — flexible monthly payment plans through our financing partners. 0% APR options available for qualified customers on installations over $1,500.

What warranty do you provide?

All installations come with our 3-year parts & labor warranty. EV charger and panel installs include 5 years. We also honor all manufacturer warranties.
