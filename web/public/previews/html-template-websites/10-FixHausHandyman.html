FAQ

Good questions,
straight answers

Everything people ask before their first booking. Still stuck? Our team replies within the hour.

Ask us anything
How is pricing calculated?

Most jobs are flat-rate by task with the starting prices shown above, plus materials. Larger jobs are quoted hourly after a free estimate. You approve the price before any work begins — no surprises.

How fast can someone come out?

Same-week availability is standard, and most bookings land within 2–3 days. Same-day and emergency slots are available for urgent issues like active leaks.

Are your handymen insured and vetted?

Every FixHaus pro is background-checked, skills-assessed and covered by our liability insurance. We also stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee.

What's covered by a membership plan?

Plans bundle scheduled inspections, priority booking, member-only repair discounts and (on Pro and Total) included handyman hours each year. You can use the credit on any service.

Do you handle emergencies?

Yes. Tag a booking as Emergency in the estimator or call our line directly. For active leaks, electrical faults or security issues we prioritize same-day dispatch.

Is there a warranty on the work?

All workmanship is backed by a 1-year guarantee. If something we fixed fails, we come back and make it right at no charge.
