FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with SuperShop.

Ask a question
How fast is delivery?

We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM in metro areas. Standard delivery takes 1-3 business days. All orders over $49 ship free.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Fresh produce and perishable items can be refunded within 24 hours of delivery if quality doesn't meet standards.

How does the loyalty programme work?

Earn 1 point per $1 spent. Premium members earn 2x points. Redeem 100 points for $5 off. Points never expire for active members.

Are products quality checked?

Every product goes through multi-point quality inspection. Fresh items are sourced from verified local farms and inspected before dispatch.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! Orders over $200 automatically receive 5% off. Business accounts get custom pricing. Contact our sales team for wholesale enquiries.
