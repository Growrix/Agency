FAQ

Common questions

Quick answers about treatments, safety, plans and guarantees.

More questions
Are your treatments safe for pets and children?

Yes. We use EPA-approved, low-toxicity formulations and Integrated Pest Management (IPM) techniques designed for occupied homes. Most treatments are safe within 1 hour of drying. Our technicians will walk you through any precautions specific to your service.

How often should I schedule pest control?

For prevention, quarterly visits work for most homes. Active infestations may need bi-monthly or monthly visits initially. Our Protection Plus plan (bi-monthly) is the most popular choice for ongoing protection.

What if pests come back between visits?

Our satisfaction guarantee covers free re-treatments between scheduled visits. If you see pests, just call — we'll be back within 48 hours at no charge.

Do you offer termite warranties?

Yes. Our termite protection comes with a transferable warranty up to $250,000 in damage coverage with annual inspections. Available with our Premium Shield plan or as a standalone service.

Can you handle commercial properties?

Absolutely. We service restaurants, warehouses, hotels, healthcare facilities, offices and retail. Our commercial program includes compliance documentation, HACCP-aligned reporting and dedicated account management.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Same-day service is available 7 days a week. Most emergency calls receive an on-site response within 4 hours. For wasps, bed bugs, and severe rodent issues, we prioritize next-business-day or sooner.
