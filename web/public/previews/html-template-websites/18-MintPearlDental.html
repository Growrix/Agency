Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Still curious? Our friendly front desk is a quick call away.

Do you accept my insurance?
We accept most major PPO dental plans and many HMO plans, and we'll verify your exact benefits for free before your visit. No insurance? Ask about our in-house membership plan and 0% financing — many treatments cost less than you'd expect.
I'm nervous about the dentist. Can you help?
Absolutely — gentle, anxiety-friendly care is our specialty. We offer numbing that genuinely works, sedation options, noise-cancelling headphones, and a team trained to go entirely at your pace. Many of our most loyal patients arrived terrified and now visit happily.
How fast can I be seen?
We hold same-week appointments for new patients and reserve daily slots for dental emergencies — often same-day. Book online in under a minute or call us, and we'll find the soonest time that works for you.
How much do clear aligners cost?
Most aligner cases range from about $2,800 to $5,500 depending on complexity, with financing from $129/month and 0% interest options. Your free consult includes a 3D scan and a precise quote — try the cost estimator above for a quick ballpark.
Is teeth whitening safe for my enamel?
Yes. Professional whitening done under a dentist's supervision is safe and far gentler on enamel than many drugstore kits. We protect your gums, use clinically-tested formulas and tailor the strength to your sensitivity — most patients feel no discomfort at all.
Do you see children?
We love treating the whole family, including little ones. Our team makes early visits playful and positive to build lifelong healthy habits — and you can book the whole family back-to-back to save trips.
What if I need treatment I can't afford right now?
We'll always give you a clear, no-pressure plan and help you prioritize what's urgent versus what can wait. With flexible monthly financing, membership savings and phased treatment options, we work hard to make essential care genuinely affordable.
