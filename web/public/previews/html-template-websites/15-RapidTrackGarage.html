Common Problems

When a small issue
becomes dangerous

Garage door systems are under enormous tension. Here's how a common fault escalates — and where we step in.

Warning sign

Broken Spring

A loud bang, a door that won't lift, or a visible gap in the torsion spring coil.

Safety risk

Door Falls Hard

Without spring tension, a 150–300 lb door can drop with no warning — a serious injury hazard.
