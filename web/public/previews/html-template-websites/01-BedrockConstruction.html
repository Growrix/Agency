Our capabilities

End-to-end delivery
across every asset class.

Six core service lines. One accountable team. From first feasibility to final handover.

01

Office towers, retail & business parks

Large-scale office towers, mixed-use business parks and flagship retail destinations. We deliver every major component in-house — structure, envelope, fit-out — giving owners single-point accountability and tighter cost control than any multi-subcontract model.

Get a quote
02

Luxury homes, apartments & communities

From signature single-family villas to high-rise apartment towers and master-planned residential communities. Our dedicated residential division brings construction finance structuring, design coordination and sales-phasing expertise together under one roof.

Get a quote
03

Factories, warehouses & logistics centres

Greenfield and brownfield industrial facilities — automotive plants, pharmaceutical manufacturing, e-commerce fulfilment centres and cold-chain logistics hubs. Engineered for maximum throughput, energy efficiency and future adaptability.

Get a quote
04

Roads, bridges, rail & utilities

Civil infrastructure at scale — from urban road networks and cable-stayed bridges to rail viaducts, water treatment plants and power transmission corridors. We have delivered over $8B in infrastructure value across four continents.

Get a quote
05

Modernisation of complex live environments

Structural upgrades, seismic retrofits, interior renovations and full strip-out-and-rebuild programmes — often while buildings remain occupied. Our live-environment protocols protect tenants, meet OSHA requirements and keep schedules intact.

Get a quote
06

One contract. One point of accountability.

Integrated design-and-build delivery means architects, engineers and contractors work from a single brief under a single contract. Faster decisions, no scope gaps, and a GMP price established earlier in the project lifecycle.

FAQ

Common questions

How are project timelines determined?

We baseline a schedule during planning using historical productivity data and risk-adjusted critical-path modelling, then track it live on site with weekly variance reporting.

How does pricing and estimation work?

We provide transparent open-book estimates with itemised cost breakdowns, contingency rationale and value-engineering options before contract.

What licensing and certifications do you hold?

We operate under ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001, employ chartered engineers and certified project managers, and maintain jurisdiction-specific licensing in every market.

How do you manage safety on active sites?

Every site runs a documented HSE plan with daily briefings, sensor-based monitoring and independent audits. We have logged over 12.4M hours without a lost-time incident.

Can you manage the full project lifecycle?

Yes. Our Design & Build service provides single-point accountability from feasibility through handover and post-occupancy support.
