FAQ

Questions
answered.

Everything you need to know before ordering.

What exactly is an HTML business profile? +
It's a single webpage saved as a file (.html) that contains your complete business profile — logo, services, menu, hours, contact info, and call-to-action buttons. It looks like a premium mobile app and works in any browser without an internet connection (except for Google Fonts).
Do I need a website or hosting to use this? +
No. The file works standalone — just share it like any other file. Send it via WhatsApp, email, or Google Drive. On Pro and Premium plans, we also host it at a real URL for free, so you can link to it from Instagram or anywhere online.
How long does delivery take? +
Starter and Professional plans are delivered within 24 hours of receiving your completed brief. Premium plans are delivered within 12 hours. If you need it faster, contact us for rush delivery options.
What if I want to update my profile later? +
All plans include the revision rounds specified. For ongoing updates (price changes, new menu items, team changes), you can purchase our monthly update add-on for $15/month, or order a one-off update for $19.
Can I use my own domain name? +
Yes. Premium plan includes help setting up the profile on your own custom domain (e.g. menu.yourrestaurant.com). You'll need to own the domain — we guide you through the DNS setup step by step.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, PayPal, and Wise bank transfer. For large agency orders, invoice payments are also available. All prices are in USD.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes. If you're not satisfied after your revision rounds are complete, we offer a full refund within 7 days of delivery — no questions asked. We're confident in the quality and want you to be 100% happy.
Do you offer bulk or reseller pricing? +
Yes. Agencies building profiles for multiple clients get significant discounts — from 20% off at 5+ profiles to 50% off at 20+. We also offer white-label delivery (the file has your agency's branding, not ours). Contact us for a quote.
