Kyoto

a thousand quiet mornings

Walk the vermilion gates of Fushimi Inari before the world wakes. A private tea ceremony, a machiya townhouse of your own, and cherry blossom drifting over lantern-lit stone. The old capital, unhurried and entirely yours.

The Serengeti

the oldest theatre on earth

Wake to the sound of a million hooves. A tented suite on the migration's path, sundowners on the savanna, and a hot-air balloon lifting you over the herds at first light. Wilderness at its most cinematic — with nothing between you and it.
