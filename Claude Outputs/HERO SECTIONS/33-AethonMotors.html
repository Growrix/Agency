Technology

Obsession,
engineered in

Every system exists to serve the driver. Nothing is decorative. Everything is purposeful.

01

Tri-motor powertrain

Three independent motors deliver 1,340 hp with torque metered to each wheel, a thousand times a second.

02

Active aerodynamics

A morphing rear wing and underbody flaps trade downforce for drag in milliseconds, reading the road ahead.

03

Carbon monocoque

A single woven carbon-fibre tub — lighter than aluminium, stiffer than steel — wrapped around the cell.

04

Active suspension

Each corner adjusts 1,000 times per second, flattening the car through corners and stilling it at speed.

05

800V architecture

An 800-volt pack recovers a charge in minutes and sustains track pace lap after lap without fade.

06

Torque-vectoring OS

The car learns your line. Software splits power across the axles and updates over the air, overnight.
