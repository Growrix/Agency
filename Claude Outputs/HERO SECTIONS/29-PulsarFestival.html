Good to know

Before you go

PULSAR 2026 runs August 14–16 at the Neon Valley Grounds. Gates open at 14:00 each day; music runs until the early hours, with the Afterglow stage going until dawn.

Every pass is a full three-day experience with access to all six stages, the art park and the market village. VIP and Platinum add elevated viewing, express entry and lounge access — compare them in the Tickets section above.

Yes — on-site camping ranges from general fields to fully serviced glamping. Accommodation add-ons unlock at checkout once you've selected your festival pass.

PULSAR is an 18+ event. Valid government photo ID matching your ticket is required at the gate — no exceptions.

All passes include free name changes up to two weeks before the event, and optional refund protection at checkout. The festival proceeds rain or shine.
