Inside the atelier

The studio

Every garment begins as a toile — a muslin draft draped, cut, and refined by hand before it ever meets the final cloth. This is where the story is written.

01 — Sketch

Croquis & line

Silhouettes drawn by hand, each one a hypothesis about how a body moves through space.

02 — Moodboard

Palette & material

Champagne against runway black, raw silk against steel — a season's mood resolved into seven tones.

03 — Toile

Drape & cut

The muslin prototype, pinned and re-pinned, until the fall of the fabric tells the truth of the design.

Notes & process
