01The Studio

Great design is not decoration. It is a way of living.

Atelier Vesper is a residential and commercial design studio built on a single belief — that the spaces we inhabit quietly shape the lives we lead. Five principles guide every commission we accept.

  • 01

    Form & Function

    Beauty is never decoration alone. Every decision must earn its place by making the space work better than it did before.

  • 02

    Human-Centered Design

    We design around rituals, not rooms — how you wake, gather, host, and rest. The plan follows the life.

  • 03

    Timeless Aesthetics

    Trends date; proportion does not. We compose interiors meant to feel as considered in twenty years as on day one.

  • 04

    Material Excellence

    Stone, timber, metal, and textile chosen in the flesh — natural materials that deepen and improve with age.

  • 05

    Emotional Design

    The measure of a space is how it makes you feel the moment you step inside. We design for that feeling first.

04How We Work

Six considered stages, from first idea to final styling

A process you can see clearly at every step — so you always know where your project stands and exactly what comes next.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    Weeks 1–2

    We begin with how you want to live. A studio conversation, a walk through the space, and a deep read of your taste and rituals.

    • Lifestyle brief
    • Site survey
    • Budget framework
  2. 02

    Concept Development

    Weeks 3–6

    The narrative takes shape — mood, palette, and the single idea that will hold the whole project together.

    • Design concept
    • Material direction
    • Spatial sketches
  3. 03

    Space Planning

    Weeks 6–9

    Every wall, sightline, and circulation path is resolved so the space works as beautifully as it looks.

    • Detailed floor plans
    • Joinery design
    • Lighting layout
  4. 04

    Material Selection

    Weeks 9–12

    We curate the palette in the flesh — stone, timber, metal, and textile chosen to age with grace.

    • Full material library
    • FF&E schedule
    • Final costings
  5. 05

    Implementation

    Months 4–9

    We manage the build and the makers, protecting the design through every site decision and delivery.

    • Contractor coordination
    • Quality control
    • Procurement
  6. 06

    Final Styling

    Handover

    The last and most personal layer — art, objects, and the details that make a finished space yours.

    • Styling & dressing
    • Art install
    • Handover guide
